Welcome to Keystone Gymnastics

Here at Keystone Gymnastics we work hard to build strong and happy kids. We offer a variety of programs including mobile gymnastics classes, playgroups, parties, special presentations and camps. Our students gain memorable experiences and self-esteem as they achieve an “I can do it!” attitude.

Our Story

Keystone Gymnastics is a small family business owned and operated out of Hailey, Idaho since 2017. Even though we are new to the area, we are not new to this industry. Keystone Gymnastics, specifically the Tumble Truck, is known for loading up our equipment and “bringing the fun” to surrounding areas. The curriculum we use for our mobile gymnastics program was created by the owner over the years and has been proven successful; even being taught in other states. Children and their parents seem to love the benefits our programs have to offer! We look forward to growing within the community and truly becoming a part of every family in beautiful Sun Valley.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help build strong and happy kids. Our name, Keystone Gymnastics, comes from the construction term “keystone.” It is the stone in the very center of an arch that holds everything together. This is what we want our program to be; the foundation that every child should have and can build upon in order to establish great confidence, high self esteem, an active and healthy lifestyle, balance, fun, flexibility and of course awesome gymnastics skills! We understand how busy life can get, so in order to achieve this goal we bring our equipment to the kids. We want to make our programs easily available and accessible to all the families in our area.

About The Owner

Chelsea is a program developer; creating curriculum, consulting and leading training sessions for children’s facilities throughout the country. Gymnastics has always been a large part of her life. She was introduced to the sport at two years old and continued up through competitive gymnastics till she began coaching at seventeen. Teaching in this industry for over a decade now, she has established herself by creating solid gymnastics curriculum, establishing and improving multiple children’s programs, organizing large events and developing training workshops. Chelsea remains a leader in the gymnastics community and continues to further herself by attending national and local gymnastics conferences. She loves what she does and can’t wait to share her passion with others!

Tumble Truck

What is Tumble Truck?

The Tumble Truck is a mobile gymnastics program provided by Keystone Gymnastics – bringing physical and educational benefits to children at daycares, preschools and grade schools. Our instructors provide a successful program of Olympic style gymnastics curriculum in a safe, positive and fun atmosphere! Children gain memorable experiences and self-esteem as they achieve an “I can do it!” attitude.
